About Capital Motors

Capital Motors is a trusted vehicle service and repair workshop dedicated to keeping your car performing at its best. With years of hands-on experience and a passion for precision engineering, we provide reliable automotive solutions for both everyday and premium vehicles.

Driven by Precision & Performance

Our workshop combines modern diagnostic technology with skilled mechanical expertise to deliver fast, accurate, and transparent servicing. From routine maintenance to complex engine repairs, we ensure every vehicle receives the highest level of care and attention.

We believe vehicle servicing should be simple and stress-free. That’s why we focus on honest pricing, clear communication, and timely delivery — so you can get back on the road with confidence.

With a strong commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction, Capital Motors continues to be a reliable destination for complete automotive care.

Our Services

Complete Vehicle Servicing

Comprehensive maintenance including oil changes, filter replacements, fluid checks and multi-point inspections to maintain peak performance.

Engine & Mechanical Repairs

Advanced diagnostics and mechanical repairs performed using industry-grade tools and genuine replacement parts.

Brake, Suspension & Steering

Electrical Diagnostics

Modern electronic scanning and troubleshooting for batteries, sensors, wiring systems and onboard electronics.
